Our Story

Krahs (/krôs/) is more than a swimwear line. We are on a mission to empower and encourage women to love themselves. We want to take the pressure off these ladies and remind them that there is no right or wrong way to wear a swimsuit.
Our suits were made to convey the message that being fierce and strong is beautiful and can also be feminine. Take a risk; take a bite out of life with confidence.

About The Founder

Theresa Marie Mingus was born in Richmond, CA, and from a very young age, she faced tremendous hardships, including her mother’s ongoing drug abuse and neglect, and an absentee father. She lived on the streets for much of her life, and dealt with things as a child that forced her to choose between two paths - to surrender, or to rise.

With resiliency and an open heart, Theresa was able to actively manifest the negativity in her life into positives.


The mission and driving force behind her brand, is about giving others the bravery to dream big, and to find a way through tough circumstances to RISE. Something she, herself, has done.


She took her big dreams to Los Angeles and soon after relocating found herself working side-by-side with two of the most iconic fashion and pop culture icons of her generation, Kim Kardashian and Selena Gomez.


During her time working with both women, she accumulated invaluable experience and insight into what it takes to design a successful brand.


Theresa is excited to be able to share her brand, and the culmination of her life’s work and dedication to rise with the world. This swimwear brand is so much bigger than just cloth, it's a representation of all of our ability to make great change in our own lives in spite of our circumstances.
